Cry64 is a collaborative music archive ran by the internet. Being a collaberative project, anyone may submit music they wish to be featured, all genres are welcome. If you wish to submit music that is your own, please email us a link along with a description of your music. All music is treated with an open mindset.

If you wish to submit music that is not your own, want to help out with the project, or wish to talk about music and meet new people, please join our discord server!
