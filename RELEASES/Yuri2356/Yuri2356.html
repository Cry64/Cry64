Yuri2356 Interview

Yuri 2356 is a self proclaimed music hoarder and owner of the “Hoping for something better label” formerly known as “Yuri's Imaginary Label”. Yuri runs a youtube channel of the same name (Yuri 2356) archiving and uploading albums on an almost daily basis. These albums are largely DnB, Jungle, Breakcore, Jcore and many more genres as Yuri doesn't like sticking to one genre or style.

"How did you get your start collecting/hoarding music?"

"God, I've been collecting music since like the Dino ages, but I've been collecting music since 2009 on my old ipod. Wasn't really much dnb. I was actually collecting a decent amount of classic rock music like Led Zeppelin, CCR, Queens, and Def the The Beatles. (I was a sucker back in my youth) and also had a decent amount of Jcore from stepmania and myspace".

"What is the main way you find new artists?

"My main way of finding new artists vary. Sometime I'll drop in onto Spotify and look for some underrated artists and go to the "what fans also like section" and go from there and download them off of deemix. Nyaa is great for an Otaku like me for anime related stuff. I sometimes go to old forums and see if they have some music too. Bandcamp is great too. I always check the new releases and see if I find anything interesting."

"I’ve seen you’ve released a few songs in the past. Is making music something you’re looking to continue?"

"Yes. Actually after listening to DDS's album rpg, I was inspired to make an ep of some sorts that contains samples of rare games. I'm still listening on how I can improve and such, and even thought of buying turntables. I also wanna DJ in my own state since Alaska basically has little to no music scene here as far as I know."

"In what capacity do you work with Dismiss Yourself?"

"To put it bluntly, I just upload vids for him. Love the homie before I even started so the fact that I get to help him is fuckin lit : ) "

"I have noticed the label has changed names. Is this rebranding indicative of the future of the label?"

Yes. I change the label name because I just thought it was corny, but I really wanna get my label going this year. I feel like I've been kinda lazy with it, and wanna start getting more artists interested in releasing stuff on the label.

You’ve said that the “Land Of Yuri’s Discord MIXX VOL.5” is the last of the series. Is there going to be a new mix series or are you not going to focus on mixes in the future?

The series is over, but what I want to do is more genre specific like sextrance, J-core, etc. Also gonna host more fests onto the channel in the future : )
